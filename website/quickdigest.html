QuickDigest AI

Turn long articles and YouTube videos into clear briefs in one click—entirely on your device.

Get it on Chrome Web Store Privacy policy

Available on the Chrome Web Store when published. Source is not distributed from GitHub.

What you get

  • TL;DR & full summary One-line essence plus structured sections.
  • YouTube transcripts Key moments, bullets, takeaways, and action items.
  • Summarize selection Highlight text and summarize only what you need.
  • Local engine Always works offline-first on your machine.
  • Chrome on-device AI Optional when your browser supports it.
  • History & export Pin favorites, search history, copy or export as .txt.

Privacy by design

No sign-in, no paid API keys, no analytics, and no backend operated by Maco Crafts. Summaries are generated locally and/or with Chrome on-device AI when available. Read the full privacy policy.
