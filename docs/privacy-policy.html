QuickDigest AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026
Extension version: 2.7.0
Publisher: Maco Apps / Maco Crafts
Product: QuickDigest AI (Google Chrome extension)

QuickDigest AI ("the Extension," "we," "us") summarizes articles and YouTube videos in your browser. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed, where it goes, and what we do not collect. By installing or using the Extension, you agree to this policy.

At a glance

  • No account required to use the free tier
  • Summaries are generated on your device (local engine and/or Chrome on-device AI)
  • We do not sell your data and do not show third-party advertising
  • Payments are handled by Stripe; we never see your card number in the Extension
  • Lifetime Pro uses license-key validation via our Cloudflare Worker; purchase emails are sent via Resend

1. What QuickDigest AI does

The Extension lets you summarize web articles and YouTube videos into structured formats (e.g. quick summary, bullet points, study notes). A free tier includes daily limits; a one-time Lifetime Pro purchase unlocks additional modes and features. Summarization runs in your browser; optional paid features use third-party services listed below only when you choose to pay or activate a license.

2. Summarization and on-device processing

When you click Summarize (page, selection, or YouTube), the Extension may:

  1. Read the active tab URL and page title
  2. Extract readable article text or YouTube captions visible on the page
  3. Generate a summary on your device using:
    • Local engine — extractive summarization that stays in the browser
    • Chrome on-device AI (optional) — when your browser and device support it, Google Chrome may process text with on-device models under Google's Privacy Policy

Article and transcript text used for summarization is not sent to Maco Apps servers for summarization. We do not operate a cloud summarization API and do not send your page content to OpenAI or other paid third-party summarization providers.

3. Data stored on your device

Using chrome.storage.local, the Extension may store locally on your device:

  • Theme and summarization engine preferences
  • Summary history (title, URL, preview, summary content, timestamps)
  • Daily usage counters for the free tier
  • Lifetime Pro: license key (after you enter it), activation status, a lightweight device identifier used for license binding, and related entitlement state

This data remains on your device unless you clear it in Extension settings or uninstall the Extension. We do not sync this local storage to our own servers.

4. Payments (Stripe)

If you purchase Lifetime Pro, the Extension opens a Stripe Payment Link in a new browser tab. Stripe processes payment information (e.g. card details, billing email) according to Stripe's Privacy Policy. The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit payment card numbers or bank details.

After a successful purchase, Stripe may share your checkout email and session metadata with our backend via a signed webhook so we can issue your license.

5. License validation (Cloudflare Worker)

When you activate a Lifetime Pro license key, the Extension sends a request to our Cloudflare Worker license API (https://quickdigest-license-api.maco70090.workers.dev) over HTTPS. The request may include:

  • Your license key (format QD-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX)
  • A hashed or opaque device fingerprint generated locally to bind the license to your browser installation

This request does not include article text, summaries, browsing history, or passwords. Processing is governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy as infrastructure provider.

6. Purchase emails (Resend)

After payment, our Worker may send your license key to the email address provided at Stripe checkout using Resend, an email delivery service. Resend processes recipient email addresses and message delivery according to Resend's Privacy Policy. We use email only for license delivery and support related to your purchase—not for marketing newsletters unless you separately opt in outside this Extension.

7. Extension permissions

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

Permission Purpose
storage Save settings, summary history, usage limits, and license state locally
activeTab Access the current tab only when you invoke summarization
scripting Extract readable page content on demand
offscreen Run Chrome on-device AI summarization when available
contextMenus Right-click actions to summarize the page or a selection
host_permissions: https://*.workers.dev/* Connect to our license validation API after purchase

The Extension includes a content script on YouTube watch pages only to show an optional summarize call-to-action; it does not run on all websites by default.

8. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the Extension, we may process:

  • License and device data — license key and device fingerprint sent to our Worker when you activate or re-validate Pro
  • Purchase-related data — email address and Stripe checkout identifiers received via webhook to issue and support your license
  • Support communications — information you voluntarily send to maco70090@gmail.com

Summarization content and browsing history are processed locally in your browser as described in Section 2 and are not uploaded to Maco Apps for summarization.

9. Information we do not collect

The Extension and our summarization flow do not:

  • Require sign-in or a Maco Apps account for the free tier
  • Sell or rent your personal information
  • Display third-party advertising or ad trackers
  • Run analytics or behavioral advertising SDKs in the Extension
  • Collect passwords, payment card numbers, or arbitrary form field contents
  • Upload full page content to Maco Apps servers for summarization
  • Request access to all URLs in the background without user action

10. No sale of personal data; no third-party ads

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use the Extension to serve third-party advertisements. Service providers (Stripe, Cloudflare, Resend) act only to provide payment, infrastructure, and transactional email services described above.

11. Data retention

  • On your device: history and settings persist until you clear them or uninstall
  • License server: license records are retained as needed to validate Pro access and prevent fraud; you may contact us to request deletion where applicable law allows
  • Email provider logs: governed by Resend's retention practices

12. Security

  • Summarization is designed to run on-device
  • License API traffic uses HTTPS
  • Stripe webhooks are verified with signing secrets stored only on our server, not in the Extension
  • Keep Chrome updated for the latest on-device AI security features

13. Children's privacy

The Extension is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

14. International users

Our service providers may process data in the United States or other countries. By using paid features, you acknowledge that license and payment-related data may be processed where those providers operate.

15. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the Extension or our practices change. The "Last updated" date at the top will change, and continued use after updates constitutes acceptance where permitted by law. Material changes may also be noted in the Chrome Web Store listing.
