QuickDigest AI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026 (v2.1.0)

QuickDigest AI summarizes webpages on your device. No account, no paid API keys, no cloud backend operated by us.

Data we process

On summarize: read tab URL/title, extract article text or YouTube captions, generate summary locally and/or via Chrome on-device AI. Content is not sent to our servers.

Data stored on device

Preferences and optional summary history in chrome.storage.local. Clear anytime in Settings.

Data we do not collect

No sign-in, no ads, no sale of data, no upload of page content to our servers.

Permissions

Permission Purpose
storage Settings and history on device
activeTab Current tab when you invoke the extension
scripting Extract readable text on demand
